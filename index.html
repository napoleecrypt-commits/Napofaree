📖 How To Play
```
1
Open the game together
Both Napolee and Fareedah open this same link on their own phones at the same time.
2
Take turns spinning
The game tells you whose turn it is. That person taps the Spin button — the wheel spins and picks a question randomly.
3
Answer privately
Both of you answer the question on your own — type it on WhatsApp, write it down, or just keep it in your head. Don't show each other yet!
4
Tap your Done button
When you're ready, tap only your own Done button — Napolee taps 🩵, Fareedah taps 💗. Once both are done, the answer boxes appear.
5
Type & Reveal
Both type your answers into the game, then tap Reveal — both answers appear at the same time so you can read each other's responses.
6
Next Round
Tap Next Round — the turn switches to the other person and they spin next. Keep going as long as you want!
📋 Rules
🔒 Questions do not repeat until all 100 have been used
🔄 You can re-spin if you want a different question — but it will be recorded as Spin 2, Spin 3, etc. so your partner knows
👁️ You cannot see your partner's answer before they reveal it
💾 The game auto-saves after every round — come back anytime and continue where you stopped
🔁 Turns alternate — Napolee spins first, then Fareedah, back and forth
📱 Both players must have the game link open on their phone at the same time to play
💡 Tips for a better game
Be honest — the questions are designed to help you understand each other better
Don't overthink — your first answer is usually the truest one
After revealing, talk about your answers — the conversation is the point
```
